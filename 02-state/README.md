# Chapter 02 - State

## Mini lecture (sports analogy)
State is the match scoreboard and player sheet.
Without it, the coach (Terraform) cannot tell who is on the pitch or what changed.

Terraform intent here:
- understand what local state tracks,
- see what drift means,
- and learn why teams move to remote state.
